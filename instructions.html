Interactions

  • Move forwards: W or left click or double tap and hold on trackpad or forward arrow key

  • Move backwards: S or right click or back arrow key

  • Move left: A or left arrow key

  • Move right: D or right arrow key

  • Move up:R

  • Move down:F

  • Pause 3D visualization: Q

  • Control orientation of camera: mouse or trackpad gestures

  • Bring scene to default view: CTRL + R
